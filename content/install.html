Status Bar

Clicking the Fire Eagle icon in the status bar will retrieve your current location (via Loki and Geode) and then update Fire Eagle with that location.

You can right-click the Fire Eagle icon in the status bar to access various Fire Eagle related actions.

Toolbar

Clicking the Fire Eagle icon in the toolbar will retrieve your current location (via Loki and Geode) and then update Fire Eagle with that location.

You can right-click the Fire Eagle icon in the toolbar to access various Fire Eagle related actions.

Tools Menu

A full assortment of Fire Eagle related actions is available in the Tools menu.
